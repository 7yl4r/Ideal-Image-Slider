What is a commercial site?

A commercial site is a either a website that makes money for you (e.g. an ecommerce website), or a website that you develop for a third party who pay you to create their site. Basically if you make money from a website that uses the Ideal Image Slider you need a commercial license.

How do I get support?

When you purchase a commercial license you will recieve a license key in your receipt email. You can use this license key to access our Support Help Desk.

Package plugin with commercial products?
